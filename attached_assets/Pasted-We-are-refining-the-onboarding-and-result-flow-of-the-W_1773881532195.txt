We are refining the onboarding and result flow of the Women Business Empires DNA App. The system is functional but several logical and messaging issues need to be corrected.

Please implement the following improvements carefully without breaking the existing routing structure.

⸻

1 Fix Result Page Messaging

On the Reveal page (DNA result page) remove any language suggesting statistical claims such as percentages.

Remove lines like:

“Only 14% of women have this Entrepreneur DNA”

Replace with the following messaging structure:

Your Entrepreneur DNA: {DNA_NAME}

Women with this DNA thrive when they build businesses aligned with their natural strategic strengths.

Most women try to build businesses that don’t match their Entrepreneur DNA.

That is why so many struggle.

When your strategy aligns with your DNA, everything begins to work differently.

Ensure the DNA name is dynamically inserted.

⸻

2 Improve Pre Login Teaser Experience

Currently the app reveals too little before asking users to log in.

Adjust the flow so users can see a meaningful preview of their DNA result before login.

Reveal page should show:

DNA name
Short identity explanation
Strength summary
Primary empire path

Example layout:

Section 1
DNA Identity

Section 2
What this means for you

Section 3
Your strongest empire model

Then show a section titled:

Continue Your Entrepreneur DNA Report

Below this section show a locked section with blur effect.

Example locked sections:

Your Business Compatibility Map
Your Growth Pattern
Your Empire Strategy Blueprint

Under the locked section show a button:

Unlock My Full DNA Report

Clicking this button should redirect to the signup / login page.

The correct flow should be:

Welcome
Intro
Quiz
Analyzing
Reveal
Report preview
Login / signup
Full report

Please verify and enforce the following routing rules:

Welcome page → Discover button → Intro page

Intro page → Begin DNA Analysis → Quiz page

Quiz completion → Analyzing page

Analyzing page → Reveal page

Reveal page → Report preview

Build a mobile-first web application called Women Business Empires.

Brand identity:
Women Business Empires
WBE
We Build Empires

This platform helps women discover what type of online business they are most aligned with based on their personality, strengths, and entrepreneurial style.

The first version should be simple, elegant, and focused on one core experience: discovering a user’s Entrepreneur DNA.

Design requirements:
• mobile-first layout
• modern, minimal, premium design
• black background
• gold accents to represent power and success
• soft pink accents matching a feminine luxury brand
• clean typography
• smooth transitions and simple navigation
• elegant progress animations

Build Version 1 only with the following screens:

1. Welcome / splash screen
Introduce the concept of discovering your Entrepreneur DNA.

2. Intro screen
Explain briefly that the quiz will analyze personality, strengths, and entrepreneurial tendencies to recommend the best online business path.

3. Account creation
Allow users to sign up with email and password.

4. Entrepreneur DNA quiz
Create a 25-question multiple choice quiz.
Each question appears on its own screen with a progress bar.

Questions should analyze:
• leadership style
• independence vs collaboration
• communication style
• risk tolerance
• creativity
• desire for freedom
• strategic thinking
• teaching ability
• motivation style

5. DNA analysis loading screen
After finishing the quiz show a short animated “Analyzing your Entrepreneur DNA” screen.

6. Result reveal screen
Assign one of the following Entrepreneur DNA identities:

Empire Architect
Visionary Leader
Influence Catalyst
Community Sovereign
Knowledge Guardian
Momentum Commander
Freedom Alchemist
Legacy Builder

This reveal moment should feel emotional and powerful.

7. Full DNA report page
Include sections:

• identity title
• rarity percentage
• personality explanation
• natural strengths
• skills to develop
• explanation of why past business attempts may not have worked
• future potential

8. Business compatibility scores
Show percentages for:

Affiliate Empire
Digital Product Empire
Personal Brand Empire
Knowledge Empire
Community Empire

9. Premium teaser screen
Explain that the platform can guide the user daily to build her empire based on her DNA.

This screen should invite the user to unlock the full Empire Journey in the future.

Technical requirements:

• build as a modern web app
• mobile responsive
• simple database structure for users and quiz results
• clean navigation between screens
• use placeholder logic for scoring the quiz results
• make it easy to expand later

Important:
Do NOT build payments, community features, AI chat, or mastermind groups yet.

Only build the Version 1 foundation and a polished user experience.

At the end, generate a preview so the app can be tested.
